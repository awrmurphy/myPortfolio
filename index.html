About Me

Hello! Welcome to the About Me portion of this page!
My name is Anthony Murphy, I am currently a student learning a broad suite of skills at Humber Polytechnic including HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
I have previously attended Humber where I touched on many subjects including C, C++, C#, Java, and Physics.
On top of my time at Humber I have over a decade of working experience in management positions with companies of varying sizes.
My personal interests include reading, listening to music, exploring nature, as well as playing video games and tabletop games.
Through this page I hope to convey my abilities and desire to grow as a developer.
